Our Journey

The Battle of The Bands is Designed to be a stress reliever to modern day high school. Corpus Christi College is becoming the first Western Australian school to offer such a program.

About Us

The Battle of the Bands is an idea created by the Student Prefect Team at Corpus Christi College. The idea was that the workload on students could get overwhelming at times, and we needed a way to effectively combat that, so we came up with the idea of Battle of the Bands.

 

A survey conducted by our leadership team told us that 89% of students got relieved of stress when listening, or playing some sort of music. Which was our top answer on how we could eliminate this stress

 

We have done this event for the last 3 years, with all of the feedback being overwhelmingly positive and critical acclaim from students about their mental health and other important issues being dealt with less stress.

 

After the event, we monitored the test results of the people that went to the event for 2 weeks. We saw a 65% (estimate) increase in test results. This is why we are hosting the Battle of the Bands this year.
